2010

Oracle aquires Sun. The illumos project is started as a fork of OpenSolaris to continue development in the community. Oracle stops publishing changes to the open source repository, effectively killing OpenSolairs.

Today

Over the last 11 years the community has introduced many new features to illumos, including a new bootloader, overlay networks, hardware virtualization (KVM & bhyve), drivers for new hardware and much more. Contributions come from individual developers and from companies like Joyent / Samsung, Oxide, Nexenta, RackTop, Western Digital, and others.
